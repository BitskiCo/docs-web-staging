title: "Create a Custom Drop Page"
---

Drops are one-time campaigns of NFT sales hosted by Bitski. Custom drop pages come with a set of templates and users can request to utilize this at a fixed cost.

These custom drop pages include the following items: 

* Native Bitski purchase experience with a Credit Card  
   * Low barrier of entry for any buyer to participate  
   * Onboard buyers to purchase via Bitski Wallet
* White glove service from ideation to web development
* A dedicated web development team to organize your Drop campaign
* 3 templatized options of storefronts with ability to request adjustments on a case-by-case basis

For further information please reach out to our team [here](https://bitski.typeform.com/to/ci0VSWXe).
